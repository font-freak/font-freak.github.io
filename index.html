Fonts for everyone.

Changing the default font on your Android device is easy with Magisk. Font Freak gives you a giant library of Bengali and English Font Modules for your every need.

Get Fonts Get Magisk

Easy to Flash, Switch and Remove

Font Freak uses the latest Magisk templates to produce font modules so you don't have to. Select a font you like, see a real preview and finalize selection before you download. Don't like it still? Uninstallation is simple with Magisk, just a reboot away.

Don't want to root your phone? You can even flash through recovery only (not-systemless). Changed your mind? Flash the default fonts and forget.

Take a Look

How it works

Download a font

Browse through a large collection of organized, regularly updated, and optimized English and Bengali fonts on the font library. Download the one you like, either for Magisk or for TWRP.

Flash in Magisk or TWRP

Flash the module in Magisk for a systemless font experience, easily remove it later. Or, if you prefer a rootless journey, flash through TWRP and forget.

Reboot device

Reboot your device for the changes to appear throughout your Android system and apps. Don't like what you see? Plenty of fonts to go through still in the font library.
